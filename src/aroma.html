Aromatherapy

Aromatherapy is the use of essential oils, which are extracted from plants. A treatment combines the healing properties of the pure essential oils with the therapeutic benefits of massage. The massage is deeply relaxing and aids the absorption of the oils the through the skin into the bloodstream and assists the elimination of toxins. Following an initial consultation the appropriate oils are chosen and carefully blended with a carrier oil.

Aromatherapy can help with a wide range of conditions including headaches, pmt, menopausal symptoms, muscular aches and pains, insomnia, eczema, digestive problems, stress and tension, depression, low immunity, help detoxify and many more.

We can also blend a range of Aromatherapy products to suit the individual for home use. Please contact us for further details.
