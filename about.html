About Me

Jenny has been practising complementary therapies since 1999. She ran a successful practice in Belper, Derbyshire before relocating to Abergavenny in 2025 to study counselling and therapeutic practices at the University of South Wales.

Jenny has many years experience working with a wide range of clients. Areas of focus have included stress management, back, neck and shoulder problems together with long term health concerns. Jenny has a particular interest in the emotional physical connection.
