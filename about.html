About Me

Jenny has been practising complementary therapies since 1999, she moved to her current premises in 2001 and has experience in treating a wide range of conditions and ailments.

Jenny has a keen interest in sport and participates in Triathlon (having done 2 Iron distance races) as well as being a keen cyclist (from club riding to long distance events).

She's also taught complementary therapies and healthy eating in Adult education.
